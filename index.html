I grew up in Gothenburg on Sweden’s west coast. In 2012 I completed my bachelor degree in Digital Media and soon thereafter moved to London.

At the very end of that same year I joined an agency called This Place as their first employee and I’ve worked here since. Today the agency has a second office in Seattle and around 40 employees. During my time here I’ve helped clients from all over with their digital products. I also had the privilege to represent our company as one of the speakers at the 2016 SXSW festival.

As a product designer my goal is to help businesses and their customers by building meaningful and easy to use products. I do this by building up an understanding of the business strategy, the customers, the technical capabilities and then execute on those insights. One of my strengths as a designer is that I enjoy coding. It has allowed me to prototype concepts and communicate more clearly with our development team. Being a product designer can be complex and challenging at times but I love the challenge and can’t think of a job I would rather do.
